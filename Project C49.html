Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are repltiles that are a part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is the most important part of communication for a chameleon.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around trees.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and interesting animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels are quite unique, and somewhat rare animals.
  • Camels can live without food and water for a long time, considering they live in deserts.
  • Camels store food in their humps, and have eyelashes to protect from sand.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas are extremely intriguing insects.
  • Cicadas usually live underground, and come out every 2-17 years depending upon the species.
  • Cicadas make quite pleasant music, which is used to repel birds.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas are cute animals, that are usually native to China and other Asian countries/.
  • Pandas usually feed on bamboo. They are usually vegetarian.
  • Pandas unique skin patterns protect them from the sun and other natural elements.

Interesting Facts About Bears

  • Bears are extremely strong animals, found almost everywhere in the world.
  • Bears are omnivorous, and it is dangerous for humans to be around them.
  • Bears are mainly of 3 types: Brown, Black and Polar.
